The Experience

More Than Just Pottery

In this immersive weekend workshop, you will disconnect from the digital world and reconnect with the tactile art of ceramics. Led by master potter Layla Ahmed, this course is designed to take you from a complete novice to confidently throwing your first vessel.

We focus on the meditative aspect of the wheel—learning to listen to the clay, finding your center, and shaping the earth with your own hands.

The Schedule

Your Journey in Clay

09:00 AM — Theory

Understanding Clay Bodies

Introduction to different types of clay, wedging techniques, and preparing your station.

11:30 AM — Practice

Centering on the Wheel

The most critical skill. Learn how to force the clay into perfect submission before shaping.

02:00 PM — Creation

Opening & Pulling Walls

Transforming your centered lump into a cylinder. The foundation of vases, mugs, and bowls.

Day 2 — finish

Trimming & Refining

Returning to your leather-hard pieces to trim the feet and refine the shape before firing.

Your Instructor

Layla Ahmed

"Pottery isn't just about making things. It's about how the act of making shapes us in return."

With over 15 years of experience in Cairo's ceramic scene, Layla combines traditional Egyptian techniques with modern minimalism. Her work has been featured in galleries across the MENA region.
